Psychology Live is a browser-based audience interaction system that lets presenters run live polls and collect animated bullet comments (danmaku) from participants — all without any server setup. Open `screen.html` on your projector, share the `client.html` link or QR code with your audience, and start engaging instantly via peer-to-peer WebRTC.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the display screen">
    Open `screen.html` in a browser on your presentation computer or projector. It connects automatically and waits for participants.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share the participant link">
    Share the URL to `client.html` — or use the QR code on the screen — so participants can open it on their phones.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run your Q&A">
    Participants select answers and submit them. Results appear live on the display screen as an animated bar chart.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Engage with danmaku">
    Participants type messages in the danmaku bar. Their comments scroll across the display screen in real time.
  </Step>
</Steps>
